Step by step
The full agreement journey
1
IT Administrator
Create a new agreement
Draft

Log in to the portal and click New Agreement from the sidebar. You'll land on Phase 1 — Raise Agreement. Fill in all laptop/desktop details and the employee's email address. Nothing is sent to the employee until you click Submit.

  • Select the device model from the dropdown (or type a new one if not listed)
  • Enter the serial number from the label on the physical device
  • Tick which accessories are included — carry bag, power adapter, mouse, VGA converter
  • Enter the employee's email address (the signing link will be sent here)
  • Optionally add the HOD email to keep the Head of Department in the loop
  • Asset number is not entered here — it will be filled in Phase 3 (IT Verification), obtained from Finance for new devices

3
Employee
Employee reviews and signs
Agreed

The employee clicks the button in their email and lands on Phase 2 — Employee Agreement. They fill in their own personal details, read the laptop/desktop summary, tick the agreement checkbox, and click Submit.

  • The link only works for you — if anyone else opens it, access will be denied
  • Fill in your Full Name, Employee ID, Position/Job Title — these are saved to the signed agreement
  • Select your Department from the dropdown list
  • Your Windows ID is auto-filled and cannot be changed
  • Review the device and accessories listed by IT admin — they are read-only
  • Tick the "I agree" checkbox and click Submit to complete your part

4
IT Administrator
Verify device & complete the agreement
Agreed → Completed

You'll receive an email that the employee has signed. Open the agreement from Existing Agreements (filter by Agreed). Scroll down to Phase 3 — IT Verification. Physically check the device, enter the Asset Number, tick the checklist, and click Complete Agreement.

  • Go to Existing Agreements → click the Agreed filter card to find it quickly
  • Confirm the employee's name and department match the person in front of you
  • Check the serial number on the physical device matches what is on the agreement
  • Enter the Asset Number — get this from Finance for new devices. This is mandatory to complete the agreement
  • Tick both verification checkboxes and click Complete Agreement

⚠️ What if something is wrong?

↩ Reject Agreement
Status goes back to Pending. A fresh signing link is automatically emailed to the employee so they can re-sign after corrections are made.
📦 Archive Agreement
Used when the agreement is no longer needed (device returned, employee left, etc.). You must type a reason — it is saved permanently to the record.

Good to know
Helpful tips
⏰

Signing link expires in 7 days

If the employee misses the deadline, go to Existing Agreements → open the agreement → click Resend to generate a fresh link.

🔒

Links are personal and secure

The signing link only works for the specific employee it was sent to. Forwarding the email will not let anyone else sign on their behalf.

🖨️

PDF available at any stage

IT admins can export a full PDF at any time — including archived agreements. It captures all phases, signatures, dates, and device details.

📦

Archiving requires a reason

To archive an agreement, IT admin must type a written reason. This is saved permanently to the record so there's always an audit trail.

👁️

Employees can view their own agreements

Employees can log into the portal and see the status of their own agreements. They will only ever see their own records — never others'.

🔄

Rejection triggers a new signing link

If IT admin rejects after signing (e.g. wrong details were entered), a brand-new signing link is automatically emailed to the employee.

✏️

IT admins can edit hardware fields at any status

IT admins can update hardware details (model, serial number, asset number, accessories) on any agreement — including Completed and Archived. Every save automatically sends a notification email.

🔕

Email notification preference

IT admins can opt out of system-wide email notifications in User Management. Admins with notifications off will only receive emails for agreements they personally created.
